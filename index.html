About Me

I am Jasmine Kaur, a SEBI-certified investor, author, and entrepreneur who thrives at the intersection of finance, technology, and innovation. My journey is defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a commitment to creating meaningful impact.

As the visionary behind Blackstone Harbor, a financial intelligence platform, and Agriloop-Lite, a sustainable agriculture solution, I blend quantitative analysis with strategic foresight to solve complex challenges.

Beyond the markets, I authored Elevated, a book dedicated to empowering individuals through resilience and purpose. My work has been recognized by the Sub-Divisional Magistrate of Khanna, honoring my contributions as a youth advocate and leader.

Core Values: Elegant intelligence. Purposeful ambition. Strategic empathy. Visionary leadership.

My multidisciplinary expertise spans financial markets, quantitative trading, full-stack development, and strategic business planning. I hold certifications from prestigious institutions including SEBI, Yale, Harvard, and Microsoft, reflecting my commitment to continuous learning.

As a polyglot fluent in four languages and a participant in global competitions including the Harvard Crimson Global Case and Essay Competitions, I bring a truly international perspective to problem-solving.

My mission is to democratize financial literacy, leverage technology for social good, and build enterprises that combine profitability with purpose.
